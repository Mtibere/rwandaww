OUR STORY

Background

Rwanda we want organization is a registered non-government organization based in Rwanda. It is built on the strong belief that if the future is to be in good hands the youth must be empowered and enabled to exploit their full potential as motors of the country’s development.

The club was a space where the youth got together to discuss matters affecting them on a daily basis including unemployment, sexual reproductive health, civic rights and responsibilities among others. The youth discussed various ways in which they could economically empower themselves using entrepreneurship as a major tool with a job creator and not a job seeker mentality.

They discussed their rights and responsibilities as citizens, exchanged skills and knowledge about what makes a good citizen and why it was important for the youth to actively participate as citizens. They also discussed issues related to youth sexual reproductive health including teenage pregnancies, contraceptive methods, and gender-based violence, how they affect the youth, cause school dropout and how they youth can collectively deal with and overcome them. As a youth from a country that has witnessed the 1994 genocide against the Tutsis which resulted in over 1 million deaths, discussions revolving around good leadership and its principles, peace, unity, and reconciliation were also prominent.

After graduating from secondary, procedures began to turn the club into a non-government organization and expanded its activities from 1 to 5 secondary schools. It now works with over 2000 youth from 3 districts of the country.

About Us

Rwanda We Want aims to train and harness leadership skills amongst young scholars, we want to provide and teach values of a good leader.
