About Us

Our mission

Empower the youth and bring them together to become pillars of sustainable development.

Our goal

Youth have in the past been the target of manipulation;it is therefore paramount engaging them in positive and Critical thinking so that they may instead become a pillar of sustainable development.

Our Vision

Having a critical thinking youth capable of designing and putting in place policies leading to sustainble peace and development.

RWANDA WE WANT

Rwanda we want organization was created in 2015, when a group of secondary school students got together with the idea to create a club in which the youth could discuss how to become good leaders of tomorrow. Primarily focused on youth civic engagement so as to empower young leaders and ensure sustainable development, it later started to tackle all the problems which may prevent the youth from fulfilling their full potential. It is then that the club took on subjects such as youth economic empowerment, youth sexual reproductive health, inclusion, peace and reconciliation. After graduating from secondary school, the club grew to become a non-government organization which was officially registered in 2018. The name Rwanda We Want was inspired by the “Africa We Want” theme that reflects a vision for Africa based on aspirations of African countries and their people.
